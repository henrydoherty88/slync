Why Sleep Matters

70% of high school and college students don't get enough sleep. Only about 1 in 4 high school students gets the recommended 8–10 hours on a school night.

Sleep deprivation directly impacts grades, athletic performance, mental health, and physical health.

Chronic sleep deprivation increases risk of obesity, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and weakened immune system.
